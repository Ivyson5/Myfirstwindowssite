The Panther

The panther is like leopard,
Except it hasn't been prepared.
Should you approach a Panther crouch,
Prepare to say Ouch.

Lines on Facing Forty

I have a bone to pick with fate,
Come here and tell me girly,
Do you think my mind is approaching late,
Or simply rotting early.

The Lama

The one_l lama,
He's a priest.
The two_ll llama,
He's a beast.
And I will bet
